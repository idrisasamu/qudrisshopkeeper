Privacy Policy for Qudris Shopkeeper

Last updated: April 2025

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all users (“you”, “your”) and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection laws and standards.

⸻

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following types of information:
	•	Account Information: Name, email address, and login credentials.
	•	Business Data: Product information, sales records, inventory, and staff data entered by you.

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless you provide it voluntarily.

⸻

2. Purpose of Collection

We use collected data strictly for:
	•	Operating and maintaining the App

	•	Processing transactions and generating reports
	•	Improving security, stability, and performance

⸻

3. Data Usage & Improvements

By using this App, you agree that Qudris Shopkeeper has the right to use anonymized and aggregated data to improve the application and provide a better experience for all users.

This means:
	•	We may analyze trends across all users (without identifying you).
	•	No personal or business-identifiable data will ever be shared publicly without consent.

⸻

4. Data Storage & Security
	•	All data is transmitted securely using encryption in transit (HTTPS).
	•	Supabase and/or secure databases are used for cloud storage.
	•	Local device storage is encrypted by default.
	•	We implement industry-standard ISO/IEC 27001 security practices.

⸻

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data.
Data may only be shared in these limited circumstances:
